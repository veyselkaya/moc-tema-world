Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

MOC Tema World has been serving quality fresh coffee to coffee lovers for many years. As one of the pioneers of third-wave coffee in Turkey, MOC manages its loyalty program through this application. This privacy policy explains how your personal data is collected, used, and protected.

1. Data We Collect

Our application may collect the following data:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Information: Profile photo (optional)
  • Usage Data: QR code scans, earned stars, order history
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Loyalty program management and star tracking
  • Sending reward and campaign notifications
  • Improving app experience
  • Providing customer support

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data may only be shared:

  • With our service providers (Firebase, Google Cloud) for technical operations
  • With authorized authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Firebase security rules
  • Regular security updates

5. Camera and Photo Access

Our application:

  • Camera: Used only for QR code scanning
  • Photo Library: Used only for profile photo selection

These permissions are optional, and core app functionality works without them.

6. Notifications

We may send campaign and reward information via push notifications. You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

7. Age Appropriateness

Our application is suitable for all age groups. We recommend that younger users use the app under parental supervision.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active. When you request account deletion, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

9. User Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

10. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced via in-app notification.
