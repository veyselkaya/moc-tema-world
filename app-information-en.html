App Information

This page provides TestFlight testers with an overview of the MOC Tema World app and its core functionality.

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. App Purpose

MOC Tema World is the official mobile app that allows MOC customers to join and use the loyalty program during coffee purchases. Users can earn stars via QR code, view rewards, and manage their account.

2. Core Features

  • Earn stars with QR code scanning
  • View total stars and reward status
  • Manage profile and account information
  • Receive campaign and informational notifications

3. Notes For Testers

  • The app follows the same core flow in both testing and production environments.
  • QR and reward flows are optimized for in-store usage scenarios.
  • If you encounter any issue, please contact our support team by email.
